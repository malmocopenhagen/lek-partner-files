# Steffen Timm

Steffen Timm is a Partner in L.E.K.’s European Healthcare practice and based in the Munich office. Steffen has particular expertise in the European healthcare provision market, cutting across all major therapeutic areas, as well as in the global MedTech arena. His focus lies on advising corporate clients and financial investors on transaction support and M&A topics, as well as on high-impact growth strategy.

Specifically, Steffen has worked extensively with large, integrated European healthcare platforms, as well as with regional providers of specialist care. He also has broad experience in working with global MedTech companies on driving organic and inorganic top-line growth, including areas such as dentistry, cardiology, and robotic surgery.

Steffen holds a first-class honors degree in political economy from King’s College London.

**Undergraduate:** Bachelor of Science in Political Economy, King's College London
