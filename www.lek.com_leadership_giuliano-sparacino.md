# Giuliano Sparacino

Giuliano Sparacino is a Partner in L.E.K. Consulting’s Transport practice, based in the Middle East. Giuliano focuses on sector strategy and corporate transformation across the transport industry. He has worked with clients across the Middle East and Africa for the past 15 years, ranging from transport ministries and regulators to sovereign wealth funds and private operators along the transport and logistics value chain. Giuliano has particular expertise in the aviation sector, both commercial and private, passenger and cargo.

Prior to joining L.E.K., Giuliano worked in Kearney transport practice in the Middle East. Before that, he was a researcher in transport economics at Bocconi University. He holds a M.Sc. in economics from Bocconi University and a Master in transport economics from Bocconi Business School.

### Education

**Graduate:** Master of Science in Economics, Bocconi University; Master in Transport Economics, Bocconi Business School
