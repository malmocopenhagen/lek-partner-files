# Larry Verge

Larry Verge joined L.E.K.’s London office more than 30 years ago.  He has deep expertise in advising organisations across the public and private sectors on matters of corporate strategy and development.  His experience extends across the economy, spanning a range of industrial and technology-oriented sectors including defense, telecommunications, IT and media.

Formerly Chief Financial Officer of L.E.K. Consulting Group, Larry focuses on supporting L.E.K.’s global finance and development agenda.

Larry received science and engineering degrees from Dalhousie University in Canada and a Master of Business Administration degree with distinction from Harvard Business School.

### Education

**Undergraduate:** Bachelor of Science and Bachelor of Engineering, Dalhousie University, Canada

**Graduate:** Master of Business Administration, Harvard Business School
