# Jon Piper

Jon Piper is a Partner Emeritus. Most recently he was head of L.E.K. Consulting’s Boston office and a member of L.E.K.’s Americas Regional Management Committee. He joined the firm in 1998 and has more than 20 years of consulting experience across the healthcare industry. Jon has extensive transaction, commercialization and portfolio optimization experience within the life sciences sector, working with clients at various stages of development. Within healthcare services, he has advised his clients on how best to achieve profitable growth, manage their costs and complete value-creating transactions. Prior to joining L.E.K., Jon was a consultant with Accenture (formerly Andersen Consulting), where he focused on healthcare services. He earned his MBA from Harvard Business School and his BA in economics from Trinity College.
